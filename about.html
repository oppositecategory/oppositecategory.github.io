About1

I'm an Applied Math student from Israel studying in Holon Institue Of Technology. I'm studying Applied Math yet my passion is for pure mathematics which I study by my own alongside the degree. I've yet to study enough mathematics to state any particular fields of interest yet I'm interested to learn more into Algebraic Geometry, Algebraic Topology, Number Theory (Both arithmetic and analytical flavors), Complex Analysis/Geometry and interplay between the fields. As for Applied Mathematics; I've studied, both from courses taken off my Institue and through self-study, a variety of useful tools and theories such as Statistical Inference, Sequential Models (The course was mainly about the Bayesian perspective), Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Signal Processing. I have my fair share of coding experience in Python and gained familiarity with the the basic "scientifc stack" of Python with libraries such as Numpy, Scipy, OpenCV, Matplotlib and etc for algorithms implementation and research purpose (I intend to upload all the code to Github soon). On top of that I participated in a research project (Similiar to the REUs being done abroad) in Random Matrices with Professor Eugene Kanzieper in my institue. The project was about showing that two statistical indicators, known to be useful in theory when having the full data about a quantum system, remain intact when there is missing data due to real-life experiment limitations. As of now we are working on putting together our results into a paper which I will publish here when ready.
